Welcome to Crystal Collector Game!


At the beggining you will receive a random number.

Each one of the four crystals have a random value - by clicking on the crystals you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hiden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal. Have fun!
